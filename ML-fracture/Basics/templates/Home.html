First Aid for Fracture:

  • Keep the injured area immobilized.
  • Elevate the injured limb if possible.
  • Apply ice to reduce swelling (use a cloth or towel to protect the skin).
  • Do not try to realign the bones.
  • Seek immediate medical attention.

Additional Tips:

  • Keep the person calm and reassure them.
  • Cover any open wounds with a sterile dressing.
  • Do not give the person anything to eat or drink.
  • Keep them warm with a blanket if needed.
